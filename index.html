Production features

firing refractory clay in a special tunnel kiln at a temperature of 1200 C. Tiles are produced in the same way as clinker bricks - clay purified from impurities is squeezed out of an extruder under high pressure , like toothpaste from a tube . Next, a special thread is used to cut the tiles into a given format , then they are dried and baked in an oven for two to three days . At the same time, the tiles are not inferior to brick in their physical characteristics - impressive moisture absorption rates of 3% and the number of frost resistance cycles of 250 make this material especially strong and durable .

There is a cardinal difference in the production of products from refractory and ordinary , in particular , Russian clay . From ordinary types of clay you can make ceramics , i.e. To . it , unlike refractory , has a different composition and its maximum firing temperature will be no more than 800 C. Ceramics are inferior to clinker in strength and durability . The range of clinker tiles for facades is very rich and amounts to several hundred different options . All are different in color , surface and format , which you can view on our website .

As for visual perception, not a single builder or architect will determine what the façade of the building is faced with - brick or tile . After all, visually we see only the surface . Modern building mixtures make it possible to glue tiles in such a way that their tear strength will be high , and the layer of clinker with adhesive mortar will be even stronger in its mass than solid ceramic bricks .

Clinker tiles for the facade - price per m2 in Moscow

The price per m 2 of clinker tiles for facades imported to Russia ranges from 50 to 150 euros per square meter . The cost is determined by two factors - production and transportation costs . Since deposits of refractory clay suitable for clinker production are not found in Russia, all products are supplied from Germany and Poland . Production is very energy-intensive - huge volumes of gas and electricity are consumed . Prices for most items are set in euros , invoices are issued at the rate of the Central Bank of the Russian Federation on the day of payment . However, the cost does not include consumables , such as special solutions for laying and filling joints . Also important factors influencing the price are format and surface .

  • Thus , tiles of standard NF format 240*71 mm will cost much less than those whose sizes have exclusive parameters - for example, for the exquisite series Riegel50 or Glanzstueck with a length of 440-490 mm you will have to pay up to 150 euros / m 2 .
  • If the surface is smooth and even - i.e. e . nothing was used in manufacturing to give a unique appearance , such tiles will cost less than an aged or glazed front side .

Clinker tiles for facades were first brought to Russia in the early 90s . Since then, it has gained popularity and is rightfully considered one of the best options for external cladding . Whereas in Europe it has already become widespread since the 1980s . years _ It was originally created and used where standard format facing bricks cannot be used - for example, when reconstructing facades and insulating buildings . But over time, it won the hearts of developers with its appearance , ease of installation and price , which is lower than the cost of solid or slotted clinker bricks .

Analogues of clinker tiles for facades

Today, brick-like façade tiles are found in several types :

  • Clinker - has a lot of different variations in color and surface . Relatively expensive when compared with domestic ceramics , which are inferior in appearance and strength . The service life is several decades without changing the color shade . Durable in terms of maintaining the original appearance . Cost from 50 euros / m2 .
  • Artificial stone is also inferior in appearance . These are vibropressed concrete tiles to which chemical color pigments are added . The characteristics will be comparatively lower due to moisture absorption of up to 10%, or even higher . Over time, such tiles will fade and become covered with small cracks . Whereas for clinker, the moisture absorption rate varies within 3-6%, because of this it almost does not absorb water and temperature differences have little effect on it . Concrete tiles can be bought for less than 1000 rubles / m2 .
  • Porcelain tile , but this material is unsuitable for use on the facade . It lacks vapor permeability . Steam passes through the walls , encountering such a surface, steam can pass through the seams , but will still tend to pass through the tiles . The result will be cracks throughout the entire surface . Clinker is a particularly durable material , but quite vapor-permeable , as it has a unique pore structure .

In addition to the above materials for facade cladding, there are also a lot of cheaper materials such as plaster , paint , siding and various types of panels . As you can see , today there are a lot of options for exterior decoration , so why do clinker tiles for facades deserve your choice ?

Why is it worth buying clinker tiles for the facade ?

The first thing you need to pay attention to is the appearance . A rich assortment of colors and textures from hundreds of options will satisfy the requirements of the most sophisticated connoisseur of a brick facade . The variety and unique depth of shades fascinates and leaves an indelible impression of the colorfulness and play of color . Klinker.VIP company specialists will be happy to assist if you want to buy clinker tiles for the facade .

Second is excellent quality . Clinker has tremendous resistance to adverse atmospheric factors - such as sunlight , precipitation , wind, and is also an excellent anti-vandal agent . This means that the facade will remain in its original condition for many years . The durability and durability of the appearance is confirmed year after year on houses where this type of cladding was chosen . The quality is also confirmed by laboratory tests , such as frost resistance , moisture absorption , and bending strength . The service life is more than 100 years , and in insulation systems based on expanded polystyrene foam or mineral wool it is about 50 years , depending on the level of installation work .

Third - unpretentiousness . The surface remains unchanged for many years . There is no need to update the walls , and there is no need for touch-up painting or repairs . Even the most textured tiles , with recesses and recesses , and filled with toppings , do not become dirty for many years . If , for example , minor stains from rain splashes are found in the basement of a building , they can easily be removed with a high-pressure washer ( for example, “Kärcher” ).

Yes , you will agree - this is a worthy cladding option that you should stop at . But what do you need to know when choosing clinker for a facade , and how , besides color, does it differ ?

Clinker tiles for facade : important parameters

  • Color : for ease of selection and site navigation, we have conditionally divided the entire color gamut into several separate color groups . Going to a page with , for example , brown clinker , you will see at least several variations of this color , with different surfaces and shades . Why are there no green tiles or bright red tiles ? As mentioned above , this product is based on special types of clay , which can be of different colors . Mostly red is common in various versions - with carbon deposits , with a white coating , differing in shade - from light to dark . But you can also find gray , close to black , sand and other colors . Why can artificial stone be almost white , but clinker tiles for facades have a maximum light shade close to ivory , slightly beige ? The fact is that only natural coloring pigments are used in production . A simple rule applies: whatever color the clay is based on , that’s what the tile will be . However, the shade is adjusted during production . Other clays , sometimes iron oxide , are added to the main composition , and the temperature is adjusted . Technological subtleties are used in terms of supplying heat and oxygen to the tunnel oven .
  • Shade : clinker is based on refractory clay - it is a natural material . It is safe to say that a facade made in this style is unique , since the color always “plays” on the facade . Shade may vary slightly from batch to batch . On the one hand , you can be sure that even if your neighbors have the same brick tiles of the same article, the color of the walls in the overall volume will always be slightly different ( including the type of masonry , the color of the joint ). On the other hand, how can you protect yourself from purchasing a color scheme that is half a tone different from the one for which the money was paid ? This is done by simply laying out on the front of the box the batch of the exact batch that is currently in the warehouse . We are always happy to provide such a service free of charge , so you will make an unmistakable choice .
  • Surface : on our site there are several types of surfaces , mainly they can be divided into : class ="bullet--two"> Smooth - follows from the name , i.e. e . smooth , regular geometric shape , but not slipping , matte .
  • Relief - with all kinds of notches and depressions , sometimes with sprinkling . Rough to the touch
  • Aged - the same as embossed , only the edges will already be torn off . Imitates antique brickwork .
  • Premium is a special type that is not included in any of the above descriptions . It is characterized not only by a special type of surface , but also includes some new products from the series of recent years .

    • It is worth mentioning separately about glazed tiles . Such a surface will be smooth , almost sliding , and the base will always be clay of red or white varieties . This outer glassy coating contains silicate fusible compounds that sinter with the base and form a durable shiny layer . Thanks to this layer , depending on the angle of incidence of the sun's rays , the color of the walls shimmers . One gets the feeling that at different times of the day and when the weather changes, the building constantly changes its appearance , like a chameleon , but within the same basic color scheme .

    • Format : if you want to buy clinker tiles for the facade , you will understand that the most common is the NF format 240*71 mm . There are variations in tile thickness from 8 mm to 17 mm . The thickness is determined by the technological features of production , for example, an aged surface cannot be produced in a thickness of 8 mm . Consumption is approximately 48 pieces / m2 , including the seam . The weight depends on the thickness , so the weight of 9 mm tiles will be 17.5 kg / m2 , 14 mm will be 25 kg / m2 . Sometimes the tiles are supplied double and built on the end side and are never separated in advance . This can be done without difficulty right before installation work . There are also other formats that are less popular in Russia , these are DF 240*52, WDF 210*65 and others , we will not focus on them , since by and large these are custom-made items . We also have long tiles .
    • Seam : according to the recommendations of manufacturers, the seam should be 10-12 mm , tiles are always sold taking this parameter into account . That is , if you need 100 m2 , this means that you will need 4800 pieces of NF- format tiles , taking into account a 10-12 mm seam . In reality , if you glue closely , this amount will not be enough for the specified volume . This is done for reasons of aesthetics and vapor transmission . Bricks are always laid taking into account the seam ; visually it looks much more impressive , and the presence of space between the tiles will make it easier for water vapor to pass through the wall . Although the clinker itself copes well with this task , this will serve as an excellent help .
    • Seam color : can significantly change color perception . There are a great variety of grout mixtures on the market , but we recommend purchasing special colored elastic solutions for filling joints for clinker tiles for facades . The so-called “grout” is selected taking into account several factors . For example , how will the mortar contrast with the tiles on the facade , should the main color be highlighted , or, on the contrary, should this not be done . The answer to this question can be quickly obtained by laying out a demo sample and applying various grouts to it to make a choice on one . Elasticity in the solution is achieved by adding certain plasticizers , which make it possible to achieve high values of physical characteristics , such as adhesion and frost resistance . A good solution for filling joints almost does not change its original color over the years , microcracks do not form on it and it is convenient to work with such a solution .
