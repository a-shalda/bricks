About R773NF14

Clinker tiles R773NF14 are produced at one of the best German enterprises - the Feldhaus Klinker plant. The letter “R” stands for “Riemchen”, which means “tile”. Aged surface, VASCU series, “water touches”. One of the most popular and successful gray colors, with a white coating, attracts with its appearance, reminiscent of antique brickwork. The basis is clay of gray varieties. The entire Feldhaus Klinker façade program is manufactured using the piecemeal method. Those. clinker tiles are not cut from the brick, but are produced as is. Both ordinary and L-shaped corner elements are produced for the design of door and window openings, as well as external corners of the building. Standard European NF format, ideal imitation of clinker brick laying. With proper installation, the service life of the facade will be more than 50 years. Supplied in cardboard boxes of 24 pieces, double, i.e. On the side, two tiles are soldered. They are easily separated with a light blow in the middle of the back side.
