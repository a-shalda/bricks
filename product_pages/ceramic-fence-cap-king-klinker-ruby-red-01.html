Description
Clinker tiles R658NF14 are produced at the German plant Feldhaus Klinker. The surface is aged, with the effect of hand molding. One of the most popular colors of all German-made clinker tiles. The uneven front side, torn edges, black firing and white coating give the tiles uniqueness and individuality. In addition to ordinary tiles, L-shaped corner tiles are produced for decorating door and window openings, as well as external corners of the building. Standard European NF format, brick-like tiles, flawless imitation of clinker brick laying. With proper installation, the service life will be more than 50 years. Supplied in cardboard boxes of 24 pieces, double, i.e. The two tiles are soldered on the side and can be easily separated with a light blow in the middle of the back side.
